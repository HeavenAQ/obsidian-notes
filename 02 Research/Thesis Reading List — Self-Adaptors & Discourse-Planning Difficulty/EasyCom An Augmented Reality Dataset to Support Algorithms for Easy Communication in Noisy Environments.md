**Background.**  AR communication in noise needs aligned multimodal data. 

**Research question / methodology.**  egocentric RGB, multichannel audio, transcriptions, voice-activity, and speaker/source labels. 

**Conclusion.**  a useful auxiliary corpus for timing, diarization, and multimodal alignment under noise; its egocentric view makes it a support corpus rather than the main self-adaptor benchmark.
