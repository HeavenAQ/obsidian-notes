**Background.**  DG evaluations can accidentally leak test information. 

**Research question.**  do pretraining and oracle model-selection choices blur the meaning of 'generalization'? 

**Methodology.**  an audit of protocols with concrete recommendations. 

**Conclusion.**  avoid oracle test-domain selection and document all tuning -- justification for fairness-first benchmarking and anti-leakage safeguards in the proposal.
