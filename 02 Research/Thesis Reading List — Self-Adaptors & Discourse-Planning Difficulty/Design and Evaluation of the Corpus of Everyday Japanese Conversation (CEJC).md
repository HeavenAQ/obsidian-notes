**Background.**  ecologically valid conversation corpora with video are rare. 

**Research question / methodology.**  ~200h, 577 conversations, ~2.4M words, 1675 speakers; video + audio + transcripts with interactional annotations (dialogue acts on the core subset). 

**Conclusion.**  the most naturalistic discourse benchmark (and Japanese) -- great for generalization stress-tests, though body visibility/framing is highly variable.
