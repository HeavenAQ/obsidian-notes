**Background.**  hand-over-face behavior was treated as nuisance occlusion. 

**Research question / methodology.**  detect and classify hand-over-face gestures plus fine-grained descriptors (hand shape, occluded facial region). 

**Conclusion.**  region-of-contact is semantically informative -- useful for designing subtype labels (chin-rest, mouth-cover, scratch) and occlusion semantics.
