**Background.**  turn-taking models are largely speech-centric. 

**Research question.**  do gaze and manual gestures aid turn-transition coordination? 

**Methodology.**  corpus analysis of face-to-face interaction. 

**Conclusion.**  discourse timing is intrinsically multimodal -- strengthens the case that audio-only discourse models are incomplete and that event timing at turn boundaries matters.
