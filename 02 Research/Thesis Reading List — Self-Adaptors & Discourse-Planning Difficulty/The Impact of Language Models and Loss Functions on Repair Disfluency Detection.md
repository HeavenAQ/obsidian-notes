**Background.**  repair detection on transcripts. 

**Research question.**  how much do language-model strength and loss-function choice matter? 

**Methodology.**  a noisy-channel repair detector with varied LMs and losses. 

**Conclusion.**  strong LM baselines are surprisingly hard to beat -- the rigorous text-only baseline that self-adaptor features must complement or outperform, enforcing benchmarking discipline.
