**Background & research question.**  detect hand-to-face touch in realistic scenes for disease tracing. 

**Methodology.**  supervised contrastive learning on RGB clips handling partial occlusion. 

**Conclusion.**  a strong, simpler 'contact in the wild' RGB baseline -- an alternative to building a full 3D contact-aware model first.
