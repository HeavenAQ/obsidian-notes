**Background.**  multimodal meeting analysis needed a shared corpus. 

**Research question / methodology.**  ~100h of synchronized multi-view video, audio, orthographic transcripts, and multiple annotation layers including dialog acts. 

**Conclusion.**  a workhorse source domain for robust turn-taking, pause, and repair-style modeling and cross-corpus generalization (CC BY 4.0).
