**Background.**  dyadic data with full body/hand/face plus transcripts is scarce. 

**Research question / methodology.**  a multi-view audiovisual dataset across tasks with varied cognitive/social demands; the v0.5 release adds transcripts and extracted body/hand landmarks. 

**Conclusion.**  a strong cross-domain foil to NoXi for transfer experiments with richer body visibility and task-conditioned analysis.
