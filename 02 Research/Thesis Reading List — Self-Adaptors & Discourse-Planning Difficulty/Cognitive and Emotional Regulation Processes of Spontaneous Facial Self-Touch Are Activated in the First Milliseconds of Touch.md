**Research question.**  what neural processes accompany self-touch onset? 

**Methodology.**  EEG replication and extension around the first milliseconds of touch. 

**Conclusion.**  cognition/emotion-regulation signatures appear immediately at touch -- the physiological defense for modeling contact events as regulation-relevant. (Note: the source list mis-titled this entry; this is the closest matching Spille et al. 2022 paper.)
