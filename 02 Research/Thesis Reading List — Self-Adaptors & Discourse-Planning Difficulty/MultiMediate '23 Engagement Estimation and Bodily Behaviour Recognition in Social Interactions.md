Recommended via tonight's paper "The NoXi Database" (Cafaro et al., ICMI 2017) as an adjacent-literature substitute, not a direct reference — NoXi is a 2017 resource paper whose citations predate 2023. MultiMediate '23 (ACM Multimedia 2023) is the direct continuation: it adds novel engagement annotations on NoXi for a controlled challenge and introduces bodily behaviour recognition using the BBSI scheme on MPIIGroupInteraction — the standard benchmark entry point for running discourse/engagement models on the corpus this thesis plans to use.
