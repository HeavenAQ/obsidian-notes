## ✅ Operating rules

Task operations live in [[00 Home/Tasks/Task Command Center]].

1. New notes land in **[[00 Home/Inbox/Inbox|00 Home/Inbox]]** first; loose tasks land in **[[00 Home/Tasks/Task Inbox|Task Inbox]]**.
2. Learning notes move to the relevant top-level study collection—[[01 Computer Vision — Foundations Study Tracker|CV Foundations]], [[01 DL Daily Lessons — Step-by-Step + Quiz|DL Daily Lessons]], or [[01 DL Homework Practice — MIT 6.7960|MIT Homework]]. Research notes go to **[[02 Research/02 Research|02 Research]]**, code/interview notes to **[[03 Algorithms/03 Algorithms|03 Algorithms]]**, and reusable explanations to **[[04 Reference/04 Reference|04 Reference]]**.
3. Images and videos are stored in **[[99 Assets/Media/Media Index|99 Assets/Media]]** automatically.
4. Keep collection dashboards/hubs small; let Bases and Dataview handle tables.
