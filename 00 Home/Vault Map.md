# 🧭 Vault Map

This vault is organized as a small Notion-style workspace with clear top-level areas.

## 🗂️ Top-level structure

- **[[00 Home/Dashboard|00 Home]]** — dashboard, inbox, daily notes, and navigation
- **[[01 Learning/Learning Hub.base|01 Learning]]** — courses, study trackers, lessons, homework
- **[[02 Research/Research Hub.base|02 Research]]** — thesis direction and paper reading
- **[[03 Algorithms/Algorithms Hub.base|03 Algorithms]]** — DSA notes and coding practice
- **[[04 Reference/Reference Hub.base|04 Reference]]** — durable technical reference notes
- **[[90 Archive/Archive Index|90 Archive]]** — old starter notes and inactive material
- **[[99 Assets/Assets Index|99 Assets]]** — images, videos, canvases, and base scratch files

## ✅ Operating rules

1. New notes land in **[[00 Home/Inbox/Inbox|00 Home/Inbox]]** first.
2. Finished learning notes move to **[[01 Learning/Learning Hub.base|01 Learning]]**, research notes to **[[02 Research/Research Hub.base|02 Research]]**, code/interview notes to **[[03 Algorithms/Algorithms Hub.base|03 Algorithms]]**, and reusable explanations to **[[04 Reference/Reference Hub.base|04 Reference]]**.
3. Images and videos are stored in **[[99 Assets/Media/Media Index|99 Assets/Media]]** automatically.
4. Keep collection dashboards/hubs small; let Bases and Dataview handle tables.
