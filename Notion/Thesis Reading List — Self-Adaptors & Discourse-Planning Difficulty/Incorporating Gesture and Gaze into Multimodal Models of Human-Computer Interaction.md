**Background.**  early multimodal HCI rarely tied body behavior to repairs. 

**Research question.**  do gesture/gaze pattern around speech repairs? 

**Methodology.**  multimodal analysis relating bodily cues to repair structure. 

**Conclusion.**  bodily behavior correlates with repair type -- direct precedent for repair-sensitive labels (repetitions vs content modifications) and multimodal fusion rationale.
